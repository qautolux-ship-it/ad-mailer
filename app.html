Email Warm-up

Gradually increase sending volume to improve inbox placement and avoid spam filters.

Warm-up Configuration
Warm-up sends low-volume test emails between your own inboxes with random delays (30s–2min). Run this daily for 2–4 weeks before launching cold campaigns. Start with 3–5 pairs and increase gradually.
Warm-up Tips
Week 1–2
3–10 emails/day per SMTP. Use personal-sounding subjects. Reply to some of them manually.
Week 3–4
10–30 emails/day. Mix real contacts. Open and star emails in recipient inboxes to build reputation.
Week 5+
30–100 emails/day. Now safe to run cold campaigns. Keep warm-up running in background.
Auto-Pause Rules
SMTPs with >40% failure rate over 10+ sends are automatically paused for 5 minutes to protect reputation.
